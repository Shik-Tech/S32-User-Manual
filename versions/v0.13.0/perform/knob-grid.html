Knobs Grid ​

Turn the encoder Turn the encoder on Perform (without pressing Select) to open the Knobs Grid — all 32 knob names in a 4×8 layout (left half = knobs 1–16, right half = 17–32).

Use it when you want to look up names without turning knobs. On the main Perform View, any knob you turn takes over the display; here you browse with the encoder only.

Moving around ​

Turn the encoder Turn the encoder to move the highlight through all 32 knobs. The overlay shows one half at a time (16 names); when you cross the middle, it flips to the other half (1/2 or 2/2 in the header).

Header mini-grid ​

The row of tiny squares in the center of the Perform header is a map of all 32 knobs — left half = 1–16, right half = 17–32. The framed cell is the knob you have selected. It is always visible on Perform View; when this overlay is open, both halves stay bright so you can see where you are across the full panel.

Close the overlay ​

ActionResult
2 s idleReturns to single-knob view
Press Back (short) Back or Press Select (short) SelectCloses immediately
Turn a knobCloses and shows that knob

While the grid is open, macro-bar animation on the underlay is frozen. The header mini-grid still tracks selection.
